Location

Currently we are running our office at Nilambur, Malappuram district. Nilambur is a major town, a municipality and a Taluk in the Malappuram district of the Indian state of Kerala. It is located close to the Nilgiris range of the Western Ghats on the banks of the Chaliyar River.

We strive to continually improve quality, reliability and service for our clients in order to justify the trust placed on us. In Kerala, where land is scarce, Triangle Designs sets the benchmark when it comes to conserving natural resources, minimizing waste generation and pollution control. While we take steps to prevent adverse environmental impact in our projects, we also stress on reusing, recycling and restoring nature and natural products, in every way possible.
